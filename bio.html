Bio

Hi there! My name is Aliza, pronounced ~ uh-lisa.

Education

My educational background consists of a Bachelors in Computer Science with a minor in Anthropology. I'm also interested in the arts such as printmaking, digital art, and design. I hope to continue to improve my media art knowledge as well as exploring avenues in which I can connect this to coding. Lately I have been considering game development, but that is broad field with much to explore still. So overall I am still looking to find my best fit. :)

Music Background

I grew up involved with music in various ways. I played violin in a mariachi band from ages 7 to 14. Although I never practiced so I wasn't particularly good. I also participated in choir from elementary to high school and continue to enjoy singing today but mostly to myself or with a ukulele. As of late, I mostly just enjoy listening to music and humming to empty rooms, but I hope to breathe life into a new relationship with music by learning more about the production and creation of it.

Hobbies and Interests

I love anything having to do with food, whether it's cooking, baking, or eating it! I love learning new languages and traveling to explore other cultures. In past adventures, I went to Beijing to study Mandarin for 3 months. As well, starting this fall I will begin learning Japanese. I also practice martial arts, such as Aikido and Iaido, in which I have learned so much about myself through self-reflection and discipline.
