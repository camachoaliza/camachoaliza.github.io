Music Business

Description

An overview of the music industry including publishing, music merchandising, contracts and licenses, retail concerns, and live entertainment. Additional examination of duties and responsibilities of producers, agents, managers, promoters, and performing artists. Emphasis on new media marketing and exposure to diverse careers and streams of income within industry. Students build portfolio in preparation for career in music industry.

Units: 3
Hours: 54 lecture
Transferable: CSU and private colleges.
Prerequisites: None
Recommended: BC placement into reading level 06.
