Projects

Feathering Grip Aid

Reduced grip strength affects certain adaptive rower’s ability to:

  • maintain contact to the oar
  • feather or rotate the oar
  • the rower’s overall comfort during extended periods of rowing
Companies have grip aids that help these adpative rower's, but also compromise the rower's safety by being too restrictive and lack quick releases in the incident of a capsize. In our project, we worked with the apadaptive rowers of Community Rowing Inc. (CRI). With our CRI partner, we created him gloves that allow a solid connection to the oar, with optimal comfort and safety. Above is the final product, designed with bright orange neoprene to increase visibility and a velcro wrap around the wrist for quick release. Both features for geared towards safety but also added comfort. The wrap around the wrist created suport and the neoprene was water resistant to ensure warmth.
