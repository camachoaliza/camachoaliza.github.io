The goal of Cars and Buses is to show the Fibonacci numbers related by the recurrence relation:
Fn= Fn-1 + Fn-2
with each "car" representing a 1-tile and a "bus" representing a 2-tile. Therefore Fn equals the number of different ways you can "park" the cars and buses. The project is still in the beginning stages of prototyping and ideation, but these are some of the Corel Drawings and the first prototype. The first prototype was made on cardboard because our client, the Math Professor, was unsure of how big she wanted the puzzle. On the prototype, the added paper and sticky-notes are details she realized she wanted to add for the next prototype. Before the next prototype, I will experiment with vinyl stickers versus plotter paper as labels and their compatibility on wood and with varnish.
